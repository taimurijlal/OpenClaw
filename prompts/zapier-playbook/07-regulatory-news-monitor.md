# Script 7 — Regulatory and Compliance News Monitor

**Audience:** GRC professionals, CISOs, DPOs • **Cadence:** Weekly

Monitors public regulatory developments across all major frameworks and jurisdictions. Essential for GRC teams and CISOs who need to know about new rules before they take effect.

**Zapier actions to enable:**
- Slack: Send Channel Message
- Google Docs: Create Document from Text

```text
Generate a weekly cybersecurity regulatory update.

Search the web for regulatory and compliance
developments from the last 7 days in these areas:

1. DATA PROTECTION:
- GDPR enforcement actions, fines, guidance
- UK Data Protection Act / ICO updates
- US state privacy laws (CCPA, CPRA, new states)
- International data transfer developments

2. CYBERSECURITY REGULATION:
- NIS2 Directive (EU) implementation updates
- DORA (EU financial sector) enforcement
- SEC cyber disclosure rules updates
- CISA directives and guidance
- UK Cyber Security and Resilience Bill

3. AI REGULATION:
- EU AI Act implementation timeline
- US AI executive orders or legislation
- UK AI Safety Institute guidance
- AI and cybersecurity intersection regulations

4. INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC:
- HIPAA / healthcare cyber updates
- PCI DSS 4.0 implementation deadlines
- Financial services (FCA, OCC, FFIEC)

For each development, include:
- What changed or was announced
- Who it affects
- Key deadline (if any)
- Impact assessment (High / Medium / Low)
- Source URL

Save as Google Doc: "Regulatory Update — [DATE]"

Post summary to Slack #grc-team:
"⚖️ Weekly Regulatory Update — [DATE RANGE]"
[Top 3–5 most impactful developments]
[Any approaching deadlines in next 90 days]
```
